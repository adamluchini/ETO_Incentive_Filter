Central Air Conditioner (15 SEER)

Discount of $100

Installation specifications: Outdoor unit is required to achieve a minimum nominal rating of SEER 15 or greater as stated by the manufacturer and listed on our qualified products list. System EER must be 12 or greater. Central air conditioner must serve most or all of the home. Limited to one incentive per residence. Residences primarily heated with a heat pump do not qualify.

Nominal SEER:Seasonal Energy Efficiency Rating of the outdoor air conditioner rated by the Air Conditioning, Heating & Refrigeration Institution (AHRI). The higher the SEER, the more energy efficient the air conditioner.

System EER:Energy Efficiency Rating of the entire HVAC system (air conditioner, indoor evaporator coil, and furnace) rated by the Air Conditioning, Heating & Refrigeration Institution (AHRI). The higher the EER, the more energy efficient the air conditioner.

On-bill loan repayment option: If you own and occupy your home and have the work performed by a participating trade ally contractor, avoid the upfront cost and pay for the upgrade via a charge added to your Pacific Power or NW Natural utility bill.
